Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Tars AI Assistant ("Tars," "Service," "we," "us," or "our"), operated by Tars AI, Inc. ("Company"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered personal assistant service.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices. By using Tars, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Phone number, name, email address, and communication preferences.
  • Messages and Content: Text messages, voice messages (transcribed), images, and other content you send to Tars.
  • Profile Information: Personal details you share during conversations.
  • Payment Information: Payment details processed by Stripe. We do not store complete credit card numbers.

2.2 Information Collected Through Google OAuth Integration

When you connect your Google account to Tars, we may access the following based on your explicit consent:

  • Google Gmail: Read, send, and manage emails on your behalf when you request email-related assistance.
  • Google Calendar: View and create calendar events when you request scheduling assistance.
  • Google Drive: Access, read, and manage files you specifically request assistance with.
  • Google Contacts: Access contact information to help you communicate with your contacts.

IMPORTANT: We only access Google data when you explicitly request related functionality. We do not continuously monitor or collect your Google data in the background.

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: How you interact with Tars, including message frequency and feature usage.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and messaging platform.
  • Log Data: Server logs including timestamps and system diagnostics.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide Services: Process your requests, generate responses, and deliver AI assistant functionality.
  • Personalization: Remember your preferences and conversation history.
  • Google Integration: Access your Google services only when you explicitly request related functionality.
  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve our services.
  • Safety and Security: Detect and prevent fraudulent activity and security threats.

4. Google API Services User Data Policy

Limited Use Disclosure: Tars AI's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

4.1 Scope of Access

We request only the minimum necessary permissions:

  • Gmail: Read, compose, send, and manage emails
  • Calendar: View and manage calendar events
  • Drive: View and manage files
  • Contacts: View contacts

4.2 Data Handling Commitments

  • Access on Demand: We access your Google data only when you make a specific request.
  • No Background Collection: We do not continuously sync, monitor, or collect your Google data.
  • Temporary Processing: Google data is processed in real-time and is not permanently stored unless necessary.
  • No Advertising: We do not use Google user data for advertising purposes.
  • No Sale of Data: We do not sell Google user data to third parties.

4.3 Revoking Access

You can revoke Tars's access to your Google account at any time:

  1. Visit: https://myaccount.google.com/permissions
  2. Find "Tars AI Assistant" in the list of connected apps
  3. Click "Remove Access"

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party service providers:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI): AI processing and response generation
  • OpenAI: Image analysis and text processing
  • Stripe: Payment processing (PCI-DSS compliant)
  • Supabase: Database services and authentication
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS): Cloud infrastructure and storage

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.

5.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

6. Data Retention

  • Conversation History: Retained for 90 days, then automatically deleted or anonymized.
  • Account Information: Retained for the duration of your account and 30 days after deletion.
  • Payment Records: Retained as required by financial regulations (typically 7 years).
  • Google Data: Processed in real-time; cached data purged within 24 hours.

7. Data Security

We implement robust security measures:

  • Encryption: All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit data access
  • Infrastructure Security: SOC 2 compliant hosting infrastructure
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and penetration testing

8. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: Request a copy of your data by texting "export my data" or emailing privacy@tarschat.ai
  • Correction: Update your information through the settings portal
  • Deletion: Delete your account by texting "delete my account" and confirming with "delete confirm"
  • Google Data: Disconnect your Google account anytime through Google Account settings

9. Children's Privacy

Tars is not intended for individuals under 13 (or 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights including the right to know, delete, and opt-out. We do not sell personal information. Contact: privacy@tarschat.ai

11. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

EEA residents have rights under GDPR including access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection. Legal bases: contract performance, legitimate interests, and consent.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy and updating the "Last Updated" date.
